Travel to Peru: Feel the Spirit of the Ancient Civilization

Peru is a country where legends come to life. Here, you can touch the mysteries of the ancient Incas, explore unique nature, and immerse yourself in a culture filled with traditions and flavors. Get ready for an unforgettable journey!

Top Attractions

  • Machu Picchu

    Hidden high in the Andes Mountains, this ancient Inca city amazes with its architecture and history. Often referred to as the "city among the clouds," every stone here tells the story of a vanished civilization.

  • Sacred Valley of the Incas

    A picturesque valley that was the heart of Inca culture. Discover ancient ruins, agricultural terraces, and bustling markets that still reflect the traditions of the past.

  • Lake Titicaca

    The highest navigable lake in the world. According to legend, it is the birthplace of the first Inca. Visit the Uru people's floating islands to witness how ancient customs thrive alongside nature.


Adventures

  • Inca Trail Trek

    A challenging journey that leads you through mountain passes, ancient ruins, and dense jungles, with the final reward being the breathtaking view of Machu Picchu.

  • Flight Over the Nazca Lines

    Unravel the mystery of the giant geoglyphs carved into the Nazca desert. Seen from the sky, these enigmatic designs of animals and figures are even more awe-inspiring.

  • Huayna Picchu Hike

    A challenging climb to the peak above Machu Picchu. The panoramic views from the top make the effort worthwhile.


Cuisine

  • Lomo Saltado

    This delicious dish combines Peruvian and Asian culinary traditions. Juicy pieces of beef are stir-fried with onions, tomatoes, and potatoes, then served with rice. A true explosion of flavors!

  • Ceviche

    A traditional Peruvian dish that will tantalize your taste buds. Fresh, raw fish is marinated in tangy lime juice, combined with finely chopped onions, cilantro, and spicy chili peppers. Served with sweet potatoes and corn, this dish captures the essence of Peru's coastal cuisine in every bite.


Peru is a place where antiquity meets modernity, and nature harmoniously combines with culture. Here you will find not only incredible sights and adventures, but also the opportunity to touch the real magic of an ancient civilization. You will remember this journey for the rest of your life.
