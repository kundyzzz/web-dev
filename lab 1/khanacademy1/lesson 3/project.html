Kimbap

Kimbap is a popular Korean dish consisting of rice wrapped in seaweed sheets (nori) with a variety of fillings. Common ingredients include vegetables, meat, eggs, and pickled radish. It is a convenient and delicious snack that can be packed for work or a picnic. While similar to Japanese sushi, kimbap differs in that it doesn't include raw seafood and is wrapped with a thicker layer of rice.

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Short-grain rice 2 cups
Nori (seaweed) 5 sheets
Spinach 1/2 lb
Pickled radish 1/2 lb
Beef 1/2 lb

Step 1: Cook the rice, then mix it with sesame oil, sesame seeds, and salt.

Step 2: Place a sheet of nori on a rolling mat, spread the rice evenly, leaving a bit of space on one edge.

Step 3: Add spinach, pickled radish, carrot, and sautéed beef on top of the rice.

Step 4: Roll tightly using the mat, then slice into bite-sized pieces.

Tiramisu Cake

Tiramisu is a beloved Italian dessert made from layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers (savoiardi), a creamy mascarpone filling, and a generous dusting of cocoa powder. This no-bake dessert offers a light, creamy taste with coffee and chocolate flavors, making it an ideal treat for any occasion.

  • Time: 4 hours
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Brewed coffee (cooled) 1 cups
Ladyfinger cookies 1 package
Mascarpone cheese 2 cups
Heavy cream 1 cups
Cocoa powder To taste

Step 1: Brew strong coffee and let it cool. Add coffee liqueur if desired.

Step 2: Dip ladyfingers into the coffee and arrange them in a cake pan.

Step 3: Whip heavy cream and mascarpone together, adding sugar and vanilla.

Step 4: Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then repeat the layers.

Step 5: Chill for several hours, then dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Tortellini

Tortellini are small Italian pasta dumplings typically filled with cheese or meat, often served in rich sauces like creamy, tomato-based, or garlic butter sauces. It's a hearty and flavorful dish that can be prepared quickly, making it perfect for both everyday meals and festive dinners.

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cheese tortellini 1 lb (450 g)
Unsalted butter 1/2 cup
Garlic 4 cloves
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cups

Step 1: Cook the tortellini according to the package instructions.

Step 2: In a skillet, melt butter and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.

Step 3: Toss the cooked tortellini in the garlic butter mixture.

Step 4: Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil, season with salt and pepper, then serve hot.

Source: culinaryhill - totellini recipe
